How It Works

For NGOs

Register your NGO

Click on Register and Connect as an NGO, fill out the form to register your organisation with us.

Receive Problem Emails

Whenever an problem is reported, if it matches one or more category that your NGO is registered under, and if it is in the same locality as your NGO, we will send you an email with the details of the problem, along with a picture.

Resolve the problem

Take up the problem and resolve it, to help make this world a better place :)

For Individuals

Click a Picture

If you find out about an problem which you would like to get addressed, resolving which could make a positive impact on the society, then click a picture of the problem.

Fill out the Form

Fill the form given below, by ticking all the categories which you think pertains to the problem, and remember to add the picture you've clicked with the address and description of the same.

Submit the Form

After you submit the form, the details of the problem along with the picture will be sent out to an NGO which is located close by.

Ready to make an impact?

So you've identified a problem, now fill out this form. Try to be as clear and precise as possible.
